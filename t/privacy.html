Privacy Policy

Description: A private API for managing and publishing content across my personal social media accounts.

Last updated: December 1, 2025

Data Collected

  • Authentication tokens and session identifiers needed to post to my own social media accounts.
  • Content metadata (captions, media URLs, scheduled times) to enable publishing.
  • Operational logs (timestamps, status codes, error messages) for troubleshooting.

Data Use

  • Data is used solely to schedule, publish, and manage posts for my personal social media accounts.
  • Logs are used to monitor delivery and resolve errors.

Data Sharing

  • No data is sold or shared with third parties.
  • Data may be shared with the relevant social platforms as required to publish content through their services.

Data Retention

  • Operational logs and scheduling data are retained only as long as needed for posting and troubleshooting.
  • Tokens are rotated or revoked when no longer needed or if compromise is suspected.

Security

  • Credentials and tokens are stored securely and not shared.
  • Access is restricted to the account owner.

Your Choices

This is a private tool; if you have been granted temporary access, you may request deletion of your data or revoke tokens by contacting the owner.

Changes

This policy may be updated at any time. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
